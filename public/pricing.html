Pricing FAQ

Is there a free plan?

Yes! The Starter plan is completely free with basic features. You can still earn SA tokens and use core platform features.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Paystack (NGN), and Flutterwave for international payments.

Can I upgrade from Starter to Creator?

Absolutely! You can upgrade anytime from your account settings. The verification fee ($25) is a one-time payment.

Do I keep my SA tokens if I cancel?

Yes, earned SA tokens remain in your wallet. You can withdraw them even after canceling your subscription.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for annual plans. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime.

What is the verification fee for?

The $25 one-time fee covers identity verification and the cost of maintaining the verified creator program.
