Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. Live chat offers immediate assistance.

I'm having payment issues. Who do I contact?

For payment-related issues, please email payments@primemar.com or use the contact form with "Payment" as the subject.

How do I report a user?

Use the "Report" button on any post or profile, or email security@primemar.com for urgent reports.

Can I speak to someone on the phone?

Yes, phone support is available during business hours. Premium creators get priority phone access.
