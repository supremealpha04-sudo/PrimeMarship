Creator FAQ

How do I become a verified creator?

Reach 3,000 followers, maintain a clean record for 30 days, and pay the one-time $25 verification fee.

When do I get paid?

SA earnings are calculated daily. Withdrawals are processed within 3-5 business days after request.

Can I have multiple revenue streams?

Yes! Verified creators earn from SA tokens, subscriptions, and boosted posts simultaneously.

What content performs best?

Video content tends to have the highest engagement. Mix of educational, entertaining, and personal content works well.

Is there a limit to subscribers?

No limit! You can have unlimited subscribers at $7/month each.

How do I promote my subscriptions?

Use exclusive content previews, subscriber-only live streams, and special perks to attract subscribers.
