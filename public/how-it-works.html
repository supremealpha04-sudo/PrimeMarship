How PrimeMar Works

Understanding the SA token economy and how you can earn from your influence

What are SA Tokens?

SA (Social Alpha) tokens are the native currency of PrimeMar. They represent the value of your social influence and engagement on the platform.

0.5 SA per 1,000 followers
80 SA daily max earnings
100 SA = $1.00

How to Earn SA Tokens

1

Grow Followers

Earn 0.5 SA for every 1,000 new followers. The more followers you gain, the more you earn.

Example: 10,000 followers = 5 SA
2

Engage & Create

Likes, comments, and shares contribute to your engagement score, boosting your earning potential.

3

Daily Accumulation

SA tokens are calculated and distributed daily, with a maximum of 80 SA per day.

4

Withdraw Earnings

Convert your SA to real money. Minimum withdrawal: $5 (500 SA) via Paystack or Flutterwave.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often are SA tokens distributed?

SA earnings are calculated daily and added to your wallet at midnight UTC. You can track your earnings in real-time on your dashboard.

Can I lose SA tokens?

No, earned SA tokens are yours permanently unless you violate platform rules. Violations may result in forfeiture of unwithdrawn earnings.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Go to Settings → SA Wallet, enter the amount (minimum 500 SA), select your payment method, and submit. Withdrawals are processed within 3-5 business days.

What happens to the 20% reserve?

The reserve is managed by platform admins for community rewards, creator bonuses, platform improvements, and emergency funds.

Is there a limit to how much I can earn?

You can earn up to 80 SA per day. There's no lifetime limit, so consistent growth leads to consistent earnings.

Can I buy SA tokens?

Currently, SA tokens can only be earned through platform engagement. They cannot be purchased directly.
